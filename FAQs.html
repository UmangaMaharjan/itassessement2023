What type of design styles do you specialize in?

Our team specializes in a variety of design styles, including modern, traditional and more. We work closely with clients to understand their preferences and design a space that reflects their personal style.

How long does the design process take?

The design process varies depending on the scope of the project, but typically takes anywhere from a few weeks to several months. We provide a detailed timeline during the initial consultation and keep clients updated throughout the process.

What types of spaces do you design?

We design a wide range of spaces, including residential homes, apartments, offices, retail stores, restaurants, and hotels.

What is your design fee structure?

Our design fees are based on the scope of the project, the level of customization required, and the estimated time required to complete the project. We provide a detailed fee proposal before starting any work.

Can I use my own contractors and vendors?

Yes, we are happy to work with your preferred contractors and vendors. We also have a network of trusted professionals that we can recommend if needed.

Do you offer virtual design services?

Yes, we offer virtual design services for clients who are not located in our area. This service includes virtual consultations, design concepts, and product recommendations.

How involved will I be in the design process?

We encourage clients to be involved in the design process and provide feedback throughout the project. We believe that collaboration leads to the best design solutions.

Do you offer a warranty or guarantee on your services?

We stand behind our work and offer a satisfaction guarantee. If you are not happy with the final result, we will work with you to make any necessary changes or revisions.
